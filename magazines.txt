1 L'Équipe (France) - More than just a sports newspaper, L'Équipe is essential reading in France for daily sports news, particularly football.
2 FourFourTwo (United Kingdom) - A global football magazine offering comprehensive coverage, including in-depth features and tactical analysis.
3 Kicker (Germany) - A staple in German sports journalism, Kicker covers both domestic and international football extensively.
4 La Gazzetta dello Sport (Italy) - Italy's leading sports daily that provides a thorough focus on Serie A and international football.
5 Marca (Spain) - A major Spanish sports newspaper renowned for its detailed coverage of La Liga and Spanish football.
