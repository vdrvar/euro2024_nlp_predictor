Euro 2024 wall chart: Free to download with full schedule and dates


By Mark White last updated 13 May 2024 

FourFourTwo's Euro 2024 wall chart is free – just download it and print it off yourself




 



 



 



























(Image credit: Future)




You're going to need a Euro 2024 wall chart, aren't you?Now that every nation is confirmed for Euro 2024, you can start tentatively planning your nation's run to the final – and for that, we can assist you with a fully-printable, A4 sheet to pin up on your wall and follow throughout the tournament.FourFourTwo's Euro 2024 wall chart will see you through the whole month: we have the full schedule, with every fixture. While you watch the tournament, you can fill in every result as it comes in.There's nothing like pinning it up on the wall and tracking your team's progress, now is there? It's easy to find out how to watch every fixture at the tournament, too, as the squads of each of the favourites battle it out for ultimate glory in Germany this summer. FourFourTwo Euro 2024 wall chart: Download at home versionEuro 2024 Wallchart (Image credit: Future)Here it is. All you have to do is click this link or the picture above to get the downloadable wall chart. It's completely free to download.Be sure to check the details on the knockout rounds. Teams from different groups will advance to different knockout matches and it's not as straightforward as it may first appear.
