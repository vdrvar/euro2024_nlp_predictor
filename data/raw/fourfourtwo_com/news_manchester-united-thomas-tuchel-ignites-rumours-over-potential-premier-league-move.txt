Manchester United: Thomas Tuchel ignites rumours over potential Premier League move


By Matthew Holt published 8 May 2024 

Manchester United have been linked with the former Chelsea boss, who is set to depart Bayern Munich at the end of the season




 



 



 



























Bayern Munich boss Thomas Tuchel.
(Image credit: Getty Images)
