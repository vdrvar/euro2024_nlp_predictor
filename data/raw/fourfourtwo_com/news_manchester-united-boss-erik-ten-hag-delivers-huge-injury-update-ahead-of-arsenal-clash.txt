Manchester United boss Erik ten Hag delivers huge injury update ahead of Arsenal clash


By Matthew Holt published 10 May 2024 

Manchester United are set to welcome back several first-team players ahead of their clash with Arsenal




 



 



 



























Manchester United manager Erik ten Hag.
(Image credit: Getty Images)
